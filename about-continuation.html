OUR MISSION

Changing the world one smile at a time.

OUR VISION

To have a world full of happy and healthy children who grow up to be productive adults free of childhood trauma.

WHAT WE’RE DOING AND HOW YOU CAN HELP:

Advocacy

We create awareness of child abuse and its implications in adulthood. We engage parents, children, teachers, and the community in awareness forums where we educate and also listen to their perceptions.

Empowerment

As an organization, we seek to end all forms of child abuse within our communities. We are passionate about empowering adolescents with both physical and verbal self-defense skills to teach them to stand up for themselves in compromising situations. Additionally, we offer trauma healing support to victims of abuse. We offer these sessions in primary and secondary schools as well as in community forums.

Donations

We mobilize and offer in kind and monetary donations to extremely poor families and also to child rescue centers. Our donations include foodstuffs, clothing, and all school requirements.

Capacity Building

We are a vibrant team of youth volunteers invested in building our knowledge and skills for employability and personal development. We conduct youth empowerment conferences where we learn both soft and hard skills. We involve all youths in our communities.

Counseling and Psychology

We enjoy visiting with children in rescue centers. During the visits, our team of psychologists spends time with children, establishing a rapport with them and conducting subsequent visits for psychological support to the children they identify to be in need.

RESEARCH

In the quest to create a safer world for our children. We are invested in conducting research on identifying gaps in parenting, children’s mental health, and talent development. Our goal is to influence policies that will improve the lives of children. We are currently conducting research on risk analysis on what dangers children face in their upbringing.
